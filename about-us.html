Our Mission

To connect enthusiastic tweens/teens looking for ways to earn extra money and gain experience with people who need an extra set of hands to help out in and around the house.


ATTENTION TWEENS & TEENAGERS

Are you an enterprising individual who loves helping others or wants to earn a little extra cash?

If so, you are on the right platform! Lil' Youth Helpers brings together passionate children like you to the people who need your help most. Click here to join our team today! This platform truly fosters your connection to the people who need you!

ATTENTION PARENTS

Are you the lucky one with an eager & hard-working adolescent who is willing to learn responsibility?

If so, you have come to the right place! With our platform, you can easily reach out to people needing help from your lil' youth helper.


**PLEASE NOTE: Certain tasks may require some degree of supervision to ensure that your child is being treated and compensated fairly.
